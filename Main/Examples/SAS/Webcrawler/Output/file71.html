SAS Customer Intelligence 360

SAS Customer Intelligence 360 merges customer-level digital data with traditional data sources to yield a 360-degree customer view. With an easy-to-use marketing interface backed by advanced analytic capabilities, SAS Customer Intelligence 360 is instantly available on the cloud. A/B test capability enables you to evaluate the effectiveness of different creative content on customers. You can measure the results of the variations in creative content and manage performance. You can derive actionable insight from digital customer behavior. Find out more about SAS Customer Intelligence 360.
